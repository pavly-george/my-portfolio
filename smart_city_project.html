Smart City Infrastructure Design

This project focused on designing a smart city network, integrating fiber optic cables and copper wires. The network connects to an Internet Service Provider (ISP) and includes smart sensors for seamless communication, enhancing the efficiency and connectivity of the city through advanced technologies.

Key Features:

  • Integration of fiber optic and copper cables
  • Design for seamless communication with an ISP
  • Incorporation of smart sensors throughout the city
  • Enhanced efficiency and connectivity through advanced technology
